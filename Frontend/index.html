About TruthCheck

TruthCheck is an independent misinformation lab blending journalism, civic technology, and explainable AI to help people verify stories with confidence.

Our Story

This project was initiated at Brainware University, and today TruthCheck supports real-world verification workflows for local journalists, fact-checking partners, and educators across the globe.

Our Principles

We prioritize transparency, human oversight, and community feedback. Every model decision is paired with editorial context so people can understand why a claim is flagged.

Who We Serve

Journalists, teachers, public-interest researchers, and everyday readers rely on TruthCheck to stay ahead of emerging misinformation narratives and keep communities informed.
